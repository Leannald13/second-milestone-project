Travel in style from Chicago to Los Angeles, passing through Missouri, Kansas, Oklahoma, Texas, New Mexico, Arizona. also known as the Will Rogers Highway, the Main Street of America or the Mother Road, was one of the original highways in the U.S. Highway System. US 66 was established on November 11, 1926.

Take in the architecture starting in Chicago; the birth place of the Skyscraper, or the Gateway Arch in St Louis'. Try a drive in movie at The Admiral Twin Drive-In, showing movies since 1951 in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Historical monuments such as Bandelier National Monument or Lincoln Home National Historic Site.

See the imfamous Grand Canyon The South Rim, providing visitors with one of the best views possible; hike some of the trails or take to the sky in a Helicopter. Petrified Forest National Park in Arizona. In its south, the Rainbow Forest is full of colorful petrified wood. It’s home to the Rainbow Forest Museum

Visit iconic American restaurants such as Pizzeria Uno, Chicago’s first deep-dish pizza restaurant. See countless traditional American diners along Route 66. Check out the Las Vegas strip and try Therapy, an amazing restauarant with unique dishes from red breakfast waffles to buttermilk chicken.
